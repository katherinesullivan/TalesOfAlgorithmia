Tales of Algorithmia

Who are we?


Tales of Algorithmia a is joint effort to bring knowledge about math and algorithms to you whilst making it entertaining. We ought to bring you easy to follow stories to show real world applications of the knowledge we present and of course get you interested. Knowing that narration is the most natural way in which we, as humans, are able to incorporate information we thought there was no better thing to do than sit, invent and write. And we hope you can sit, read and enjoy.


Here you can see our tales if you like the title press the read button you might as well like the story. Cheers!
